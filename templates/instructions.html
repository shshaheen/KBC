Rules And Regulations:

Questions

The KBC Quiz consists of 15 questions.

Each correct answer will move you to the next question, increasing your earnings.

Wrong Answers

A wrong answer will cause you to fall back to the starting point of the current safe zone and lose any earnings beyond that point.

Prize Money

Each question has a fixed prize amount, starting from Rs. 1000 and going up to Rs. 1 Crore.

The prize amount increases with the difficulty of the questions.

Safe Zones

There are Three safe zones where you can secure your winnings:

  • Rs. 10,000
  • Rs. 3,20,000
  • Rs. 50,00,000

Lifelines

You have three lifelines available during the game:

Only one lifeline can be used per question.

Each lifeline can be used only once during the quiz.

About Each Lifeline

  • 50-50: Two incorrect options will be removed.
  • Switch the Question: The current question will be replaced with a new one.
  • Shield: Even if your answer is wrong, it will be treated as correct.

Quit

Quit button will be enabled after first Question
